Job Title: Data Analyst — HR Analytics
Location: Remote (India / EMEA overlap)
Department: People Analytics

About the role
We are hiring a Data Analyst to join our People Analytics team. You will partner
with HR business partners and the talent leadership team to turn employee data
into decisions about retention, hiring, and engagement.

Responsibilities
- Build dashboards in Power BI and Excel to track attrition, time-to-hire, and
  engagement KPIs.
- Run ad-hoc analyses on HRIS data (Workday) to answer business questions.
- Develop reusable Python pipelines (pandas, numpy, openpyxl) for monthly reporting.
- Apply basic machine learning (scikit-learn) for attrition and churn prediction.
- Translate findings into clear narratives for non-technical stakeholders.
- Collaborate with the data engineering team on SQL data models.

Required skills
- 2+ years of experience in data analytics, ideally in HR analytics or people analytics.
- Strong Python (pandas, numpy) and SQL skills.
- Hands-on experience with Excel (pivot tables, vlookup) and Power BI or Tableau.
- Working knowledge of statistics and exploratory data analysis (EDA).
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills.

Nice to have
- Experience with HRIS platforms (Workday, BambooHR, SuccessFactors).
- Familiarity with scikit-learn, machine learning, and feature engineering.
- Exposure to cloud platforms (AWS or GCP) and version control with Git.
- SHRM or PHR certification.
